Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best time to visit Crescent City?
A: The best time to visit Crescent City is during the summer months of June, July, and August when the weather is mild and the days are long.
Q: Is tipping customary in Crescent City?
A: Yes, tipping is customary in Crescent City. It is customary to tip 15-20% at restaurants and cafes.
Q: Is it safe to drink the water in Crescent City?
A: Yes, the tap water in Crescent City is safe to drink. The water is sourced from local reservoirs and is treated to meet state and federal standards.
Q: What is the currency in Crescent City?
A: The currency in Crescent City is the US Dollar. Credit cards are widely accepted at most businesses, but it is a good idea to carry some cash for small purchases.
Q: What is the time zone in Crescent City?
A: Crescent City is in the Pacific Time Zone (PT). Daylight Saving Time is observed from the second Sunday in March to the first Sunday in November.
Q: Will I need a passport to travel to Crescent city?
A: No, you will not need a passport to travel to Crescent City if you are a US citizen. Crescent City is located in California, so you will only need a valid form of identification to travel there.
