Weather

Crescent City has a mild climate with cool summers and wet winters. The average temperature in the summer months is around 60 degrees Fahrenheit, while the average temperature in the winter months is around 50 degrees Fahrenheit. Because of these temperatures, the best time to visit Crescent city would be during the summer in late June, July, or August.
